Experiment 2 - Living Impressions

Description

Impression of a sunny day on the Santa Cruz beach.

Technical

Uses random, noise, and sin to create movement and generated placement for the waves and sailboats, giving it a 'live' feeling.

Reflection

Due to stress, power outages, and creative blocks, it took me a long time to finish this project. I'm not fully proud of it, but I did like the movement I added to it. If I had more time, I would probably flesh out the interactivity a bit more
